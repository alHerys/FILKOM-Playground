About Us

We are students of the Faculty of Computer Science (FILKOM) from Brawijaya University. We are a community of driven and creative learners, committed to improving ourselves, along with advancing our local communities. Together, we hold passions for every sector of computer science and information technology, from developing cutting edge software, to kickstarting groundbreaking research, we have it all!

What Are We Doing?

We believe that every FILKOM student has potential to create something amazing, to create something that has the potential to change how we interact with technology, and of course, to create something which will create a difference in our community. Our mission is to create a platform, on which these talented individuals may showcase their brilliant inventions, to share with the world, and to inspire other students to create.
